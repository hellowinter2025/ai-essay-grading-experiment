Executive Summary

The main pattern is generous grading for medium and low essays, especially in the 25-point continuation task. High-level essays were slightly under-scored near the ceiling.

The clearest calibration issue: continuation essays targeting 17 points were graded at an average of 21.89, a positive bias of 4.89 points.
